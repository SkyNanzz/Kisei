Community Rules

General Rules

  1. Be respectful to all players regardless of skill level or background
  2. No hate speech, harassment, or discriminatory behavior
  3. English only in public chat channels
  4. No advertising other servers or communities
  5. Listen to staff members and respect their decisions

Griefing & PVP Rules

  1. Griefing is strictly prohibited (destroying others' builds)
  2. PVP is only allowed in designated areas or with consent
  3. No spawn camping or trapping players
  4. Do not steal items from other players' bases
  5. Use of hacked clients or cheats is forbidden

Roleplay Rules

  1. Stay in character in roleplay zones
  2. No god-modding or power-gaming
  3. Develop your character's story over time
  4. Respect other players' roleplay scenarios
  5. Keep OOC (out of character) discussions minimal in RP zones

Consequences

Violations will result in warnings, temporary mutes, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on severity and frequency. Staff reserves the right to take action based on the situation.

Appeals: Contact a staff member on Discord if you believe you were wrongly punished.
