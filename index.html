Community Rules

Respect Others

Treat all players with respect. No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination of any kind will be tolerated.

No Griefing

Do not destroy or tamper with other players' builds without explicit permission. Use claims to protect your builds.

No Cheating

Using hacked clients, cheats, or exploits that give unfair advantages is strictly prohibited and will result in a ban.

Chat Guidelines

Keep chat appropriate for all ages. No spamming, excessive caps, or advertising other servers/services.

Staff Authority

Respect staff decisions. If you have concerns about moderation, contact a higher staff member through proper channels.

Have Fun!

Minecraft is about creativity and enjoyment. Help create a positive environment where everyone can have fun.
